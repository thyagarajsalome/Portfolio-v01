About

I'm a 3D artist and Frontend Developer with over 12 years of experience. My background spans various creative disciplines, including product design, architecture, interior design, thematic environments, lighting, exhibitions, and pavilion design.

I'm passionate about leveraging my skills to create a user-friendly web application for home design. This project combines my expertise in both 3D art and front-end development. Users will be able to select products or browse by category to explore various design options and visualize them within a 3D environment. I'm currently in the learning phase of using React and Three.js to bring this concept to life.
